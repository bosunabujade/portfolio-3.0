About Me

Welcome, my name is Bosun and I am a Front-End Developer!
After graduating with a double major in Sociology/ Criminology and Writing/Rhetoric, I struggled to find what I wanted to do with my career. Originally, I planned on law school, but something was always holding me back. Somethings to note about me is that I love learning, problem-solving, and any reason to be creative. Which is why law school was never the right choice for me because it lacked the creativity I crave in life.

So, one of the main reasons I believe I was drawn to become a developer is that it encompasses all three. Developing allows me to create beautiful projects from scratch challenging skills and testing my creativity!

Outside coding you can either find me having a full conversation with my television while binge-watching a show or performing a full-on concert for my furniture.
